A Message from Trinity

Greetings From Trinity!

Dear Model United Nations San Antonio Delegates, Sponsors, Participants, and Supporters,
As an extension of our long-standing professional development school partnership with the International School of the Americas and the North East Independent School District, Trinity University is extremely proud to host Model United Nations San Antonio! We hope you will have an amazing experience on the Trinity campus. We believe that our campus offers a vibrant location for your delegating experience and for meaningful collaboration with students from across San Antonio, Texas, the nation and the world, just as it does for our university students from across the US and internationally.
If you have any questions while you are here, please do not hesitate to ask me, members of the Department of Education, our Conferences and Special Programs team, our admissions office, or anyone else you meet on campus (sadly, our students are still on their winter break). We all look forward to supporting your Trinity experience and cheering on MUNSA!
Sincerely,
Professor Angela Breidenstein
Department of Education
