Conference Schedule

Wednesday Jan 7, 2015 Printable Schedule
  • 5:00pm
    Registration
    Laurie Auditorium

    Sponsors are able to check in their delegates and grab some food before heading to Opening Ceremonies. Dinner is optional.

  • 7:30pm
    Opening Ceremonies
    Laurie Auditorium

    Join us in ISA fashion for welcoming a great conference. Dr. Luis Martinez will be speaking, so you don't want to miss it!

  • 8:30pm
    First Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Partake in the first session of MUNSA by hearing the inital speakers and starting debate.

Thursday Jan 8, 2015
  • 10:00am
    Second Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Continue Speakers List and allow for moderated caucuses.

  • 10:30am
    Sponsor Meeting
    Skyline Room

    Sponsors will partake in meeting with members of MUNSA Secretariat.

  • 11:15am
    Break
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Get to know your fellow delegates. Not just by their country name.

  • 11:15am/pm
    Staggered Lunches
    Mabee Dining Hall

    Delegates can enjoy a lunch catered by Trinity University during their designated lunch period.

  • 11:30am
    Third Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Continue moderated caucuses and allow for unmoderated caucuses.

  • 12:00pm
    Break
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Depends on the lunch schedule of the committee rooms.

  • 12:00pm
    Fourth Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Moderated and unmoderated caucuses will continue. Delegates may begin drafting resolutions.

  • 12:45pm
    Break
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Get to know your fellow delegates. Not just by their country name.

  • 1:00pm
    Fifth Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Continue to draft resolutions and open debate to consider said resolutions. Resolutions may be passed and some committee rooms can move onto the next topic in this session.

  • 5:30pm
    Dinner (School Choice)
    Mabee Dining Hall

    School sponsors have the option to choose among a variety of restaurants for their delegates to eat at.

  • 8:00pm
    Delegate Dance and Social
    El Tropicano Hotel

    After a long day of delegation, delegates can unwind and let loose at the delegate dance.

Friday, January 9th, 2014
  • 10:00am
    Sixth Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Continue to discuss resolutions. Some committee rooms may continue debate on their second topics.

  • 11:15am
    Break
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Take a break from delegating and socialize.

  • 11:15am/pm
    Staggered Lunches
    Mabee Dining Hall

    Delegates can enjoy a lunch catered by Trinity University during their designated lunch period.

  • 11:30am
    Seventh Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Committee rooms delve further into debate by opening moderated caucuses.

  • 12:00pm
    Break
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Get to know your fellow delegates.

  • 12:15pm
    Eighth Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Moderated caucuses and unmoderated caucuses will continue. Resolutions will start being drafted.

  • 12:45pm
    Break
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Get to know your fellow delegates and take a break from solving the world's problems.

  • 1:00pm
    Ninth Session
    Respective Committee Rooms

    Resolutions are considered during this time and session will eventually adjourn.

  • 4:00pm
    Dinner, Scavenger Hunt, and Trinity Info. Session
    Mabee Dining Hall

    Delegates can enjoy a dinner catered by Trinity University during this period.

  • 6:15pm
    Closing Ceremonies
    Laurie Auditorium

    Concluding ceremonies as well as announcement of delegate awards.
