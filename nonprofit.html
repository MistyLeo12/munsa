Together We Can Make a Difference

The goal of MUNSA XIX is to raise $4,500 to provide 30 entrepreneurs with micro loans. Through out the year MUNSA XIX Secretariat will host fundraisers to reach our monetary goal. All MUN clubs and members are invited to participate in and attend these fundraisers, especially our 2nd annual MUNSA Fun Run in October. Secretariat will award the MUN club that has the most active presence at fundraisers at MUNSA closing ceremonies. Join ISA and the MUNSA team help make an impact in the countries represented at the conference. This is part of our outreach effort, so contact Kate Gilbert for any questions or if you'd like to help.
