About

Registration has Closed.

Dear Sponsors,

On behalf of the Secretariat and the entire MUNSA staff, I welcome you to participate in MUNSA XIX: Visionary. This year, MUNSA will be held on the Trinity University campus from January 7-9, 2015. Registration for the conference will open August 27, 2014 and close December 6, 2014. This year’s cost per delegate is $60. Schools may register after the deadline; however, a late registration fee of $10 per delegate will be applied to the cost. The school registration fee is $100, and there is a $50 fee per sponsor. The registration fees for this year’s MUNSA are higher than last year’s conference; this is due to increased expenses of the venue as well as food.

The conference hotel will be the El Tropicano, located in downtown San Antonio. Tax exemption is only available for Texas schools who present a tax exempt form prior to the El Tropicano. MUNSA will not be responsible for reserving hotel rooms. Each sponsor must make reservations on an individual basis. To receive the special group discount, you must identify as being with the Model United Nations of San Antonio. The hotel will not be responsible for monitoring attendees. Please reserve hotel rooms for the conference in advance in order to receive the reduced rate. During the conference, we will provide lunch on Thursday as well as lunch and dinner on Friday. We will not provide dinner on Wednesday night during registration, but we will offer a meal that you can pre-order on the registration form; the cost will be $9.00 per person. The pre-ordered dinner will be served at Mabee Dining Hall on campus, and it will be a buffet that is vegetarian friendly.

On Thursday night, we will be holding a delegate dance in the Coronado Ballroom at the El Tropicano from 7-10PM. Pre-registration tickets for the dance will cost $5 per person, and tickets at the door will cost $10 per person. All proceeds will go to Opportunity International. Opportunity International is a non-profit organization committed to combating poverty by providing microloans to citizens in lesser developed countries, providing local economic stimulation as well as employment for residents. Pre-purchase conference t-shirts with your registration. We will not have any t-shirts to purchase at the conference. The t-shirts will cost $15 each; proceeds also will go to the Opportunity International.

This year at MUNSA, we will be offering a new committee for delegates to participate in: the International Atomic Energy Association. This committee will discuss and debate global issues regarding safe and secure nuclear technologies. Before the conference, the MUNSA staff will also provide a delegate training day at the International School of the Americas at the Lee High School campus on Saturday, November 15, 2014, from 9 am - 12 pm. The training day will cost $15 per person, and lunch will be provided. I am delighted to work with every school, and if any sponsor has questions, please contact me at ehoward8701@stu.neisd.net.

Regards, Eva Howard Under-Secretary-General of External Affairs MUNSA XIX
